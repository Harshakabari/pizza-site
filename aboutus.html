About Octant Pizza

"Octant Pizza is an innovative and multi-cuisine buffet restaurant in Vadodara. Octant Pizza offers unlimited 'Chhapan Bhog' meal. We serve more than 56 choices of Italian and Chinese accompanied with other cuisine like in fast food, south Indian, Punjabi, Chaat counter (make your own chaat). We also serve a wide variety of deserts. Including this, we almost serve 70-80 varieties of food including Soft drinks & Deserts. We already have 6 outlets in Vadodara. Franchises available in Gujarat, Rajasthan, Madhya Pradesh & Maharashtra. We have an attractive and well-developed plan to survive in the market."

Products and Services

"We serve Chinese, Italian, South Indian, Punjabi. The food items include Pizzas, Shakes, Sandwiches, Ice Creams, Dosa, Mini Uttapam, Parathas, Aalu Sabji, Chole, Paneer Sabji, Jeera Rice, Dal Fry, Biryani, Chaat Counter."
